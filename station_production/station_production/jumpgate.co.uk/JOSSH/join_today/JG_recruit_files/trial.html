Trial

The latest client downloads and mirrors can always be found on the JOSSH.com Support page. An updater is included with the client install, which will keep your game client updated automatically as game patches are released.

Microsoft's DirectX is also required for Jumpgate - you can always download the latest version from Microsoft.com. It is also recommended that you update your system drivers before playing, especially your video card drivers. If you are not comfortable doing this yourself, in many cases you will be able to do this automatically using Windows Update, or with a program like DriverMAGIC.

You can begin the secure account setup process immediately by visiting the Jumpgate Recruitment Center.

Once you have set up your account, you will begin a 14-day free trial period. At the end of the trial period, your account will expire unless you choose to subscribe.

To learn more about Jumpgate, and begin participating in the Jumpgate community, please take a look at the following links:

  • JOSSH
    The official site of the US Jumpgate Server, complete with pilot lookups, squad information, detailed ship and equipment listings, real-time market information, background story, and more. This website is just as much a part of Jumpgate as the game client itself.

  • Official Jumpgate Forums
    The official Jumpgate forums. Membership is for pilots only, and an account will be created for you at the same time that your game account is created.
