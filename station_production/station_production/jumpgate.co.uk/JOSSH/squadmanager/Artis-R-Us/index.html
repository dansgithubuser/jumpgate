Directive
We are for the most part Non-Combatants and wish no one pilot or faction ill will. Our primary goal is the search for and retrival of space wreakage and artifacts. We make our artifacts available for purchase at our sales stores in `The Dark Fork` and `Primus Point.` Our artifacts are priced lower than at most stores! We wish to sell our artifacts, not to store them.
