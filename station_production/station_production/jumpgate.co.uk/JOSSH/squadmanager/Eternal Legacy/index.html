Eternal Legacy "Integrity, Ingenuity, and Unity"
Directive

Ages past before the great collapse, there stood an order of knights like no other. An order, subject to no king and no faith, but only to the single guiding principle of "Do what must be done for the good of the people." In time this neutral dedication to unity and the good of all mankind led them to become sought after as guards for nobles and diplomats, and they became legends among all as "protectors of the people". With the great collapse however, like so many other brilliant stars in the heavens, these legends vanished in the mists of time.

With the great collapse came a new enemy, a new evil force, bent on dominating and destroying the people of TRI... the Conflux. With the galaxy in need of heroes, a lone pilot destined by fate discovered a DSS in the depths of space bearing fragments of data ... a story of a long ago order of knights and their dedication to the people they served. With more and more DSS came more and more information of this long forgotten group and armed with most of it`s history, Pilot ShadowHart went on to reform "The Knighthood of the Order of the Eternal Legacy".

The war has been vicious and our losses have been great, but still the Knights of Eternal Legacy struggle on, combating the evil conflux queen and her minions, led by the traitorous Thrice Seven, in a war that could spell salvation or destruction for all mankind.

Unity: Knights of all peoples dedicated to the protection of all peoples from the conflux hordes.

Integrity: Upholding honour and restoring chivalry to the universe.

Ingenuity: Doing what must be done by what ever means necessary or imaginable to accomplish our mission - the destruction of the conflux menace.
