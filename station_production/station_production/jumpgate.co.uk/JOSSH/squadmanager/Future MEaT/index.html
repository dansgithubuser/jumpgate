Directive


Future Meat Enterprises is the largest supplier of alternative meat products in the known sectors. Committed to delivering the best in quality alternative meat products, we own and operate our own Conflux agitation facilities, matter amalgamation plants, Conflux rendering orbitals, waste restructuring complexes, grow-out ranches and distribution systems throughout Octavius space and operations are overseen from the administration complex on Cinatus.

From corned-flux and deviled-xenomorph to reclaimed luncheon meats, prevenient variable entrees and corn dogs, Future Meat Enterprises offers more than 930 different items to meet a wide range of consumer preferences. To most Octavius, Future Meat is not only familiar, but synonymous with quality, identified by such trademark brands as Frosty Gristle Gel(TM), Amazing Liquid Meat(TM), MultiMeat(TM) squeezable lunchmeat, and Sanguine(TM), a non-nutritive protein alternative characterized by its reddish hue. Over the years, customers have come to expect only the best from Future Meat. With a long history of delivering lyophilized, locally processed meat products, Future Meat has long been thought of as everyone`s "hometown" meat alternative.
