OUR BEACON POLICY
2008.3.1.9:36.16
NEW BEACON POLICY is as follows:

GREY BEACONS - For economic reasons they are fair game in any space. Our pilots understand the risks with tuning even a single beacon in another faction`s space and do so at their own risk, even if they just want lower taxes.

QUANTAR BEACONS: To be tuned/flipped to Green. Any pilot that tunes/flips them non-green becomes target. Such offending pilots may be ripped at our discretion.

TUNED OCT/SOL BEACONS: Regardless of colour tuned, may be flipped at our discretion. When we are conducting military opperations, whatever sectors for which we require beacon relay information we will flip. Enemies will not be permitted to hide, even in the space of factions with whom we may not be at war.
