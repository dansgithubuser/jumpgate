Press Release 2010.10.13

As of 2010.10.13 the Amber Flame burns brightly again.

One of our agents infiltrated the Solrain Military and struck at the heart of Quantar by destroying the damaged Tempest Naval Yard at approximately 20:42 Pulsar Time.

QSSP pilot Baius was in the process of repairing the Naval Yard when the first barrage of the nuclear strike hit and his ship was destroyed in the blast.

This was done by the members of Amber Flame for the Glory of Hyperial - and we will strike again.
