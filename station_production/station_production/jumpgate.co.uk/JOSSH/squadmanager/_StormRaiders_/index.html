Directives
2008.3.23.6:51.0
Beacon Maintenance Policy:
All StormRaider pilots will be able to perform basic beacon maintenance work for their faction or a foreign faction only from its untuned state (gray) to the correct color of the faction space that the beacon is at, with a few exceptions.

Amananth and Hyperial:
As their respective BCU`s dont exist yet, you will be able to tune to the color of your choice, only if the beacon is untuned.

Unregulated space:
As Beacons provide Swarm location information, we will tune unregulated beacons to receive early warning signals of Conflux presence.
