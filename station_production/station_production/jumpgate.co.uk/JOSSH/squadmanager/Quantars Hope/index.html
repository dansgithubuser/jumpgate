The Path of Hope
2008.1.30.6:18.29
Following the Path of Hope is the initial journey to Dakhdhaat. Thy faith and truth will be tested, only the disciplined will succeed.

The Dakhdhaat is the sign of promise, training commences, without the faith and discipline proven, the Dakhdhaat will fail. The successful Dakhdhaat will be the example to follow, for the Dakhdhaat will be skilled in Heathen Cleansing.

Mulul`Abkhary` is the `Wise One`, one who is sought when the mind is not clear.

The MadBongoSmoker is the the highest order for Cleansing Heathens. Leaving constantly smoke wherever they are from Heathen ship debris cleansed in the purest way. Only the elite achieve Pure Cleansing.

Dalakham is the highest order of Hope, a master, and high member of Council.

Pilgrimmage is a path chosen by few after the Path of Hope. During this path, many things can be discovered, yet discovery is the key. Returning from Pilgrammage is impossible to measure. Only the well-prepared Pilgrim may return, one day, there is always Hope.
