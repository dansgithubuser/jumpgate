2006.1.13.10:30.42
President Vanken has returned to his rightful place. Amongst the Hyperial community! After a long and painful abscence of memory loss and drifting through space, Vanken has finally gotten his memory back. After a, by eyewitnesses confirmed, trip from Amananth to Hyperial station where he was supposed to be delivering alcoholic substances that were meant for the bar on C-deck.

Upon arrival Robax, who was on duty as barman, noticed the face of his former old leader of the hyperion force called Hyperion Inquisition. Knowing Vanken suffered from amnesia, Robax immediately contacted the local hospital and authorities to come pick up one of Hyperials greatest revolutionairy supporters.

After two weeks of intense usage of sofisticated techniques, the scientific staff working at Hyperials most advanced hospital found the cure to bring back Vanken`s memory.

Without any hesitation Vanken realised he had lost alot of time and let some of his fellow compagnons in his battle for Hyperial justice standing in the cold. Vanken Immediately proceeded to deck C4 otherwise known as the restricted area to the more exclusive quarters on the Hyperial Station. Upon arrival at the 30 feet high steel door vanken took his Order of Topaz medal of his vest and flashed it infront the sensor on the right. "ACCESS GRANTED, Welcome back Vanken!" echoed through the corridors on C-deck. As soon as the doors slowly creeped open, Vanken rushed off to his personal quarters and locked himself in for the amount of 24 hours. Suddenly his door slided open astonishing everyone that was around. Standing in a brandnew Hyperion orange/grey/black outfit with Hyperion Task Force on the back, Vanken was ready to go for it again!

Ever since the word has been spread around the Hyperion base on every single deck. Vanken has restarted his squadron for the love he shares with every true Hyperion. The Hyperion Task Force is BACK!
