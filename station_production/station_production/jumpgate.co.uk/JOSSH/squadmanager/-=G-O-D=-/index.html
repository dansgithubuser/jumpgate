Directive
General Order and Delivery is primarily an Octavian contract hauling squad. Our mission is to have fun doing the things we enjoy. PVP is not our focus but we will defend Octavian Space and our selves. We will not take agressive actions that put us at war with others. Our priority is to protect each other and anyone in the squad who wants to fight should always be willing to cover our pilots who do not fight. Contact LyteFoot if you are interested in joining and building a great team.

Rules of conduct
2004.1.18.6:58.8
  • Hauling will be done with civilian tags.
  • Make decisions that are good for business. If challenged comply with the challenging pilot as best possible. If a demand exceeds losses then take the loss if it is less than you would lose then meet the demands.
  • PvP will be conducted for defense only. We do not go attacking in other faction space or creating hostile situations.
  • PvP is not required of any member.
  • Priority one is to protect each other.
  • If fighting and the battle becomes grossly overwhelming to the other side then leave the combat. Ganking is not fun for anyone and our primary goal is to have fun without ruining someone else`s fun.
  • Pursuing an attacking force into their own faction space is necessary at times. However we do not actively participate in an attacking force focused on creating hostile situations.
  • This squad only purchases equipment from the producing station, if you are not sure check JOSSH or contact command staff.
  • We will not retune beacons of other faction`s space.
  • Beacons are taxes, taxes are profit. We will aggressively tune Octavian space to a proper red frequency.
  • Sector claiming will not be done along trade routes. We do not want to make our tows open targets needlessly.
