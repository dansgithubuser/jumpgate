Rules of Conduct/ROE:
2007.2.21.19:10.56
As a member of SOLIDARITY you are bound by these Rules of Engagement:

We engage only in consensual PvP.

Consent is assumed for enemys of Solrain flying MT in Sol Space for whatever reason. Multi-factional squads that are friendly towards Solrain and Solidarity will not be engaged.

Consent is also assumed for anyone tuning Solrain beacons green/red or running MT missions against Solrain.

Consent is assumed for any non-Solrain pilot caught stripping stations. Solrain pilots will have the luxury of a warning and time to put back the items taken.

We engage the enemy at our discretion.

We will not purchase non-produced gear from factional stations in bulk.

We will give at least 4k distance from launch tubes whenever possible.

We will never intentionally: cheat, disco, lag, exploit bugs, or splash.

As a member of Solidarity you can expect to have support regarding leveling up, finances, equipment, and knowing that we have your back. We are a committed squad with the same goals and ideals.

All members are required to have teamspeak and to register at our forums. We use our forums for squad related and event related topics and your feedback is not only appreciated but necessary.

Being an active member and contributing to our squad goals is very important to us and we need you to be here. As a rule, make it a habit to check the map for any grey beacons and tune them before doing anything else.
