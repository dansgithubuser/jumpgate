Before the Collapse

In a distance galaxy light years away from now know space in the remote parts of the Keharn space rage the wars of light and dark. Groups of the Almarri pirates raid and pillage the citizens of the Keharn sectors, leaving whole stations and outposts desolated. Nearing the end of Keharn and its way of life they formed a group of knights to combat the growing threat. Putting the call out to all Faithful Keharns to return home for training and then war. Governor Traynor en knighted the first of what was to be know as the knights Order protectors all things good. Knights order vanquished the pirate threat and continued to protect Keharn for many 1000s of years until the great collapse.

Post the Collapse

With Keharn destroyed and all they had known the last survivors of Knights Order boarded a transport ship and headed to the nearest galaxy. When they woke after hundreds of years of hyper sleep they wake to a galaxy they had never seen or heard of. They entered a area of space know as TRI, formed of the Quantars, Solrain, Octavians, Hyperions and Amananth. Their transport docked at wake station. After long awaited contact with new citizens of TRI they soon learned that the collapse spread across not only their home of Keharn but the universe. Learning of TRI`s threats of pirates and conflux the last remaining knights formed the knights order with the official support of TRI. They recruited new Octs, Sols and Quantars training them in the old ways of the knights order. Knights Order continue today to provide TRI and its citizens protection for all its enemies.

Knights Order`s communication Centre can be found at www.KnightsOrder.co.uk
