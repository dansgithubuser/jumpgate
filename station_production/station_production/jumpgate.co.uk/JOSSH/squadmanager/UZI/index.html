History
2007.7.10.16:49.59
Out of the ashes of the Great Collapse came the Zealots of The One True Way. Following in the Ancient Traditions that had been passed down by their Brethren for generations before them. We gathered steadily after The Great Collapse and watched with care as the Factions grew in numbers. As the first Squad formed in the JumpGate Beta, U.Z.I. has always set a standard of excellence for others to follow. As a Brother or Sister of the Sacred Fellowship of The Zealots, you will begin your journey toward that excellence. Come, be a part of something rare and treasured!
