OAS Code/ROE
2006.2.22.14:37.49


NAP
OAS will not engage these squads in Reg/Unreg space unless:
- They fire upon OAS ships
- Are hostile in Oct space(attacking fellow Octs in our space)
- Flipping Oct beacons

OAS would expect squads we have an NAP with to defend their own space/beacons. OAS members recognize that they can/will be fired on by sqauds we have an NAP with if they are hostile/flipping beacons in another factions` space.
NAP will be terminated under the following conditions:
- Unprovoked attack on OAS Civ/HG ships
- Civ-ripping of OAS ships

Hostilities
OAS will engage any HG ships from these squads in the defense of Octavian space/ships/interests. OAS will not usually invade another factions` space, but we do reserve the right to invade under certain circumstances.

Any squad that wishes to end hostilties with OAS should contact a member of the Command staff.

OAS will remove a hostile squad from "Hostilities" should said squad no longer pose a threat to Octavian space/ships/interests.

Declared War
OAS will activily seek to engage all HG ships from these squads in Reg/Unreg. OAS will invade other factions space in pursuit of these squads.

Squads that OAS are at war with will only be removed from this status upon surrender of said squad. Any squad wishing to be removed from "Declared War" should contact the Command staff.

Rules of Engagement (ROE)

- HG vs. HG ships only (Regardless of Unreg/Reg space)
- Only Ships level 20 and above unless hostile
- Tows/Miners (Non-combatant ships) will not be engaged in Unreg/Reg space
- No flaming on any comm. channels (f1, f3, f4, f5) or forums
- No PoD (Pirating) EVER
- No Civ-ripping EVER
- No station stripping EVER
- Having fun

The members of OAS play JG first and foremost to enjoy the game and each other in the squad, and that means enjoying all aspects of the game. OAS has strict ROE so we do not engage those who do not wish to fight. OAS respects everyones right to play the game as they see fit. Our hope is other squads will respect our desire to do non-pvp activities (towing/fluxing/mining/atry hunting) in Reg/Unreg, and will respect our non-combatants as OAS respects yours.
