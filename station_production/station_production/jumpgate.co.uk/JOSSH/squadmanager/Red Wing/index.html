Directive
Internal power struggles have left our glorius empire with fractured leadership and frequent famines. We have inherited a position with poor protection from the conflux hordes. We suffer from low morale and distrust amoungst our people. While other factions have their issues, too, one rises above all others and overcomes all obstacles. They work together tirelessly, even in the face of adversity, to ensure prosperity for their people. They are strong and united in their Brotherhood. They are role models for Honour and Glory. We have seen The Light! Together our squad members will light a new fire in Octavia founded in the teachings of Quantar. We will be the example of the new way. We will work tirelessly. We will work together. We will work with purpose. We will be the example. We hold The Light! We will not follow fractured leadership and instead give our people a new path to follow. We will stamp out famines and replace them with Strength and Abundance. We will break the bonds of distrust and regrow Pride and Honour. We will not rest. We bring forth The Light!
