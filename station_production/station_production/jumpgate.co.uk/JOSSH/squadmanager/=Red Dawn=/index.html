Squad Info
103.2.24.22:2.23
Welcome to =Red Dawn=. If you looking for a squad with a lot of rules, this isn`t the squad for you. As a =Red Dawn= member you are welcome to fly solo and do your own thing. Becoming a member of =Red Dawn= squad does not require you to do what we want. We do have a basic Code of Conduct listed below which we try follow.

Our goal is to build a squad of pilots who are looking to have fun and help each other out. There is power in numbers and in =Red Dawn= the most important philosophy is to:

Protect and help all members within the squad.

We have several veterans who can help you will all aspects of Jumpgate. Plus you will have the support of the squad against pirates, get help with fluxing, mining, artifact hunting, etc. We are now recruiting and looking for pilots of all ranks. If you have more questions about our squad, just ask anyone of our pilots or just send me a private message in Jossh`s forums.

If you are interested, please apply.

Code of Conduct
103.1.17.13:32.54
We are not here to start wars, but we also want to gain experience in PvP and in all aspects of Jumpgate. All =Red Dawn= squad members should follow the Code of Conduct listed below:

1. The first rule is common sense, but the most important. Be courteous to other pilots. Even if you are killed by another pilot in Jumpgate, which will happen, be courteous. As a general rule, it is just best not saying anything or just tell them good fight.

2. Do not kill civilians unless fired upon, especially pilots lower than level 15. We want to keep new players in Jumpgate. Only under extreme circumstances should civilians be fired upon.

3. =Red Dawn= will protect any of its squad members if attacked.
